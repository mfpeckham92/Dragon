Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is SpaceX Dragon?

A: SpaceX Dragon is a private spacecraft developed by SpaceX to transport cargo and crew to the International Space Station (ISS).

Q: When was the first SpaceX Dragon launched?

A: The first SpaceX Dragon, known as Dragon 1, was launched in December 2010.

Q: How many crew members can Crew Dragon carry?

A: Crew Dragon, or Dragon 2, can carry up to seven astronauts to the ISS.

Q: What are the key features of SpaceX Dragon?

A: SpaceX Dragon is known for its autonomous capabilities, reusable design, and advanced technologies.

Q: How can I stay updated on SpaceX missions?

A: You can stay updated on SpaceX missions by visiting the official SpaceX website or following SpaceX on social media platforms.
